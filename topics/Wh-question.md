# Wh-Question in Japanese

## 1. List of words
* 何 `[なに]`、なん: **What**
* どの: **Which**
* 誰 `[だれ]`、どなた: **Who** 
* どこ: **Where**
* どちら、どっち: **Which direction**
* どんな: **What kind of ...**
* どう: **How**
